### About me

I'm a software engineer based in Milan, Italy, focused on building reliable full-stack systems and secure architectures.

My background combines:

- production web platforms with **TypeScript**, **React**, **Next.js**, **Django**, and **NestJS**
- cloud and infrastructure workflows with **AWS**, **Docker**, **Nginx**, and **GitHub Actions**
- cybersecurity and research experience in **blockchain**, **proxy re-encryption**, **distributed storage**, and healthcare data integrity
- recent systems-programming work with **Go** and **C++20**

Currently, I’m especially interested in **distributed systems**, **cloud-native infrastructure**, **secure backend design**, and **performance-oriented C++/Go development**.
